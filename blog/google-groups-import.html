Importing all posts from the Google Groups forum

Michiel de Jong

This is just a quick update to let you know that I'm working on importing all the posts from the Google Groups forum into our database.

The goal is to have a user interface right here on tosdr.org, where all participants can submit their data points directly into the database, so we can review them much more efficiently than when we have to copy each forum thread into the database manually, as we have been doing up till now.

The review process will not change, we will still discuss all review points before we publish them on the site, but it will become more efficient.

For now, everybody can just keep on sending review points to the Google Groups forum as described on our Get Involved page, but the long-term goal is to remove that step from our process. More news soon!
